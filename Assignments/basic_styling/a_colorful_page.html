The stick's method

On International Environment Protection day, our math teacher together with the 8th graders used some laws of mathematics in nature.

  • To measure the height of a tree with a stick, we followed these simple steps:
    • Found a flat area near the tree
    • Held a straight stick vertically
    • Aligned the stick's bottom with the eye level
    • Moved away from the tree until the stick's top aligns with the tree's top
    • Measured the distance from where the student stand to the tree's base
    • That distance is an estimate of the tree's height.
